What is Belka?

Belka is for German speakers who use German all day and Russian at home — and have quietly noticed their Russian hasn't grown in years. Belka brings that second language back into adult life, with the words you actually need for politics, work, books, paperwork, relationships, your health.

The mascot is Belka (Белка), a squirrel.

Principles

Local-first. No account, no email, no cloud. Your progress lives on your iPhone.

Small and honest. No ads, no trackers, no advertising IDs, no third-party analytics SDKs.

Not a beginner course. Belka starts where your Russian actually is — with topics that matter to you today.
